About Boychild Afrika

\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0

\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0 Boychild Afrika is a dynamic empowerment organisation dedicated to uplifting young men and boys who are often overlooked and unheard in society. Founded in 2023 by Charles Saningo, the organisation began as a grassroots movement at Egerton University, advocating for the needs and rights of young men within the academic environment.\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0

\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0

\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0 Although activity briefly paused in April 2024 due to member dispersion during the long holiday, Boychild Afrika has maintained a strong presence in Kakamega and is now expanding its impact to Narok County.\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0

    \ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0 We aspire to collaborate with relevant organisations to create pathways for internships and job placements, helping young men transition into gainful employment and economic independence.\ \'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0\'a0
